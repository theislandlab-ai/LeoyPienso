# 🦉 Leo y Pienso (I Read and I Think)

**Improve your child's reading comprehension through metacognition.**

A free, open-source, ad-free web app for children aged 6-10 to learn *how they understand* what they read.

> 🧠 **Core insight:** Skilled readers constantly ask themselves questions while reading. This app teaches that inner dialogue in an explicit, playable way.

---

## ✨ Demo

[leo-y-pienso.app](https://leo-y-pienso.app) — works in any browser (Chromebook, tablet, phone). No installation, no accounts, no personal data.

---

## 📖 For parents and teachers

Each session lasts **5-10 minutes** and follows this structure:

1. **Before reading** — The child predicts what the text will be about.
2. **During reading** — Text in segments with strategic pauses: think-aloud, questions, comprehension traffic light.
3. **After reading** — Literal, inferential, and vocabulary questions.
4. **Reflection** — The child identifies which strategies they used and watches their "reader's toolbox" grow.

**Two levels:**
- 🔵 **Explorers (ages 6-8):** 50-150 word texts, audio support, picture-based questions.
- 🟢 **Adventurers (ages 8-10):** 150-400 word texts, inferences, summaries, explicit reflection.
